# Appearance
Axengrav is a Dragonborn, so their body is mostly humanoid, with scaly red skin. Each of their 8 fingers have a claw at the end, though filed down as they have no use for it being sharp. 
Their face is draconic, with sunken yellow eyes, a short snout and sharp teeth. However, despite their potentially frightening appearance, their face rests in a welcoming and pleasant smile. They have two large black horns curved backwards., and across their head and face there are scars. 
They're usually wearing a loose-fitting button up shirt , and pants in shades of dark green.
They sport a multitude of Jewellery, a pendant with a big green gem, many horn-rings and piercings on one eye, both ears and their draconic lips.
