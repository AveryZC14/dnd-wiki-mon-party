A naive young yuan ti halfblood by the name of [[Keigh'ik Vocalle|Keigh'ik]] enters the [[Scaramouth]], asking to become an adventurer! however it's clear that he would not survive long as his street smarts seem to be more like gutter smarts. Our unnamed adventuring party returns from the most boring commission ever, helping a tiefling get his cat unstuck from a tree or something. The party agrees to let Keigh'ik on a trial mission after trying to scam him, and plans to meet up at 7:30 the next morning to get the jump on the new commissions for the day. The next morning, Keigh'ik and [[Valley]] are the only ones on time, and they gather to find that every single commission is too hard for them :(. All of a sudden, [[Dewey Shirelon]] busts in, pleading someone to help him get his kidnapped son back. An elven duo, ([[Jacques Mclauren|Jacques]] and [[Jacquelynne Mclauren|Jacquelynne]] Mclauren of [[House Mclauren]]) attempt to scam the poor man into generational debt, and are interrupted by our party. Valley tries to threaten them, and is immediately interrupted by Keigh'ik, who knows of the mclauren's wealth and influence. After introducing himself and talking up the mclaurens, they leave. The party agrees to help mr Shirelon. At the [[Dewey and Linda's Smiths and more|Shirelon Household]], it seems like a brawl has broken out inside the shed, where the blacksmithing and artificing happens. Surprisingly, nothing was stolen. As Keigh'ik looks upon a massive shield, he gets a flashback of the day he told his parents he didn't want to accept his role of waging [[House Vocalle]]'s future wars, and wanted to be an adventurer. His parents were displeased. The Shirelons tell the party of the "protectoball", a contraption to keep their son Billy safe, and lend the party with a magical chisel used for breaking metal. Outside the shirelon house, there are massive claw marks heading south, so the party goes south.

along the way, they encounter an encampment of goblinoids, which they'll have to pass to cross the river. The party fights these goblinoids head on, waging a tough battle against the bugbears Charglar and Klax, the returning goblin [[Kerry]] and hobgoblin leader Idris. After striking down Idris, Knocking kerry out and persuading Klax to kill Charglar (somehow??), Keigh'ik takes Klax out for a walk as they reignite the fire of morally wrong cruelty and decapitate idris and burn his and charglar's bodies, Traumatising Kerry once again. The party continues south. Our party approaches the ruins of a town, and a stone structure probably once used for barracks. They find two strange draconic creatures, one young white dragon that seemingly has patches of brown feathers, and a drake with strangely metallic scales. These creatures are chained together, and it seems the protectoball closed itself around the chains. During a ruthless battle, Hyacinth is downed, Vally takes many hits and Baldrick sneaks behind and breaks billy out, Keigh'ik is able to talk to the creatures, calming them and promising to break them out. The drake steals the t bone steak and flees north, but Valley is able to befriend the Dragon, who's name is BX302. He likes the idea of fishing with valley, but unfortunately it is too warm for him, so he heads south. Billy is brought back to the Shirelon household, and Keigh'ik is accepted as the 4th member of the adventuring party. Another W in the bag!
