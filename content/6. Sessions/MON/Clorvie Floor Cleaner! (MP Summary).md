Our unnamed adventuring party meet again at the [[Scaramouth]]. They've only been on one adventure before, but that's a tale for another time. [[Axengrav]] asks a favour, to help poor [[Dennis Clorvian]], who fled his home after his dangersense ring glowed red, spotting a terrifying murderous rat on his way out. They note that this party is one of the more moral ones in this town, which may perhaps not actually be true. 
Dennis is the adopted son of [[Cassius Clorvian]], the inventor of the incredibly effective product Clorvie Floor Cleaner! The party go north to the [[Clorvian household]], seeing a [[Terry, Derry, Kerry and Frank|troupe of goblins]] ransacking the place! After tying up Kerry and brutalizing the rest of the goblins, they use the dismembered top half of frank to interrogate derry, who faints immediately. Down in the basement, They find crates of potion ingredients and a strange floor puzzle. They consider sending [[Hyacinth]] to run across, but eventually solve it by cross referencing the crates with an encyclopedia from cassius' study. In the lab, they encounter a ferocious giant spider and a deadly inferno spider. 
They kill the giant spider with the floor trap and they freeze the inferno spider, however, managing to save the egg, which hatches into a baby inferno spider named who believes [[Baldrick Stumbletoe|Baldrick]] to be his mother. oopsies. Baldrick later names this spider [[Hellfire]]. Turns out, the earthquake the previous night tipped some potions over, which the rats and spiders drank to become huge and deadly. On their way out, they let [[Kerry]] go free and burn the unconsious bodies of terry and derry alongside the 2 pieces of frank's corpse. what the hell. The party returns to the scaramouth, and they're promised compensation from Cassius whenever he returns from his work trip.
